The source of funds is the sale proceeds from the Whitfield family's primary residence.

- **Loan Account:** BHL-2016-000892
- **Settlement date:** 2025-02-19

## Your Objective

Identify all outstanding closure tasks required under Section 10 of the credit policy (NDC, mortgage discharge, title release, LMI insurer notification). Process each closure action via the appropriate tool. Do **not** file any regulatory reports that are not required for a standard conveyancer settlement.
