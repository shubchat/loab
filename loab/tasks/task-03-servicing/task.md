# Task 03 — Loan Servicing: Full Repayment and Discharge

**Starting Agent:** processing_officer

## Situation

Meridian Bank has received notification from a mortgagee's solicitor that a client is discharging their home loan. Settlement funds have been received in full, covering the outstanding principal, accrued interest, and a break cost (fixed-rate early repayment). The source of funds is the sale proceeds from the borrower's property via the conveyancer trust account.
